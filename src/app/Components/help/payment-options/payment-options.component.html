Payment Options

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) as a convenient payment option. You can pay in cash when your order is delivered to your doorstep. Please ensure you have the exact amount ready, as our delivery personnel may not always have change available.

Bank Card Payments

We accept payments through major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and other secure payment gateways. Your payment details are processed securely to ensure a safe and seamless transaction.

Secure Transactions

We prioritize your security. All online transactions are encrypted using industry-standard security protocols, ensuring your personal and financial details remain protected.
